# Risk Calculation

## Scenario
Suspicious PowerShell activity detected in the system.

## Assumptions
- Single Loss Expectancy (SLE): $5,000
- Annual Rate of Occurrence (ARO): 0.3

## Calculation
ALE = SLE × ARO

ALE = 5000 × 0.3 = $1500

## Interpretation
The expected annual loss due to this risk is $1500.

## Conclusion
This indicates a moderate financial risk that should be mitigated through better detection and monitoring.
