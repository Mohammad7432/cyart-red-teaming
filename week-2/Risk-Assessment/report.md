# Risk Assessment Report

## Executive Summary
A risk assessment was conducted based on suspicious PowerShell activity detected in the system. The analysis evaluated the likelihood and impact of the threat and calculated the potential financial risk.

## Objective
To assess the risk associated with PowerShell-based execution techniques.

## Risk Identification
- Threat: PowerShell misuse
- Technique: ExecutionPolicy Bypass
- Source: Internal system activity

## Risk Analysis

### Quantitative Analysis
- SLE: $5000
- ARO: 0.3
- ALE: $1500

### Qualitative Analysis
- Likelihood: Medium
- Impact: Medium
- Risk Level: Medium

## Risk Treatment
- Mitigate by improving detection (Sigma rules)
- Monitor PowerShell activity
- Restrict execution policies

## Conclusion
The identified risk is moderate and should be mitigated through improved monitoring and security controls.
